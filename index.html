Welcome to my webpage


Hello and welcome to my webpage. I am Ana-Maria Marcu, a Graduate Engineer in Arup. I have an MEng in Electrical and Information Sciences from University of Cambridge where I obtained my Masters thesis under the supervision of Professor Abir Al-Tabbaa. Throughout my career, I have been fortunate to contribute to a variety of research areas, ranging from statistical signal processing, to graphene, to computer vision and deep learning. In Arup, I have worked on applying computer vision to identify cracks in underground construction. My work on assessing the impact of COVID-19 on the economy has been used in tackling the pandemic, about which you can read more in The Times and the Arup website.
